Refund Policy

We aim to provide high-quality digital services. Since website development involves dedicated time and customization, refunds are limited and evaluated individually.

Refund Eligibility

  • Refund available only if project work has not started and request is raised within 24 hours of payment.
  • Domain and hosting charges are non-refundable once purchased.
  • If unsatisfied with design, revisions are offered — not refunds.

Non-Refundable Cases

  • Design shared or development work started.
  • Client delays due to missing content or approvals.
  • Client changes mind after project start.

Refund Process

Email refund request to gapwebinfo@gmail.com and include:

  • Registered email ID
  • Order ID / Invoice number
  • Reason for refund

Timeline

If approved, refund will be processed within 2–3 business days.
